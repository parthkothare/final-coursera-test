Diet


Diet plays a major role in maintaining good health - 'We become what we Eat.' Adopting a balanced diet helps us to gain maximum nutrition from the food we eat and aids in a harmonious functioning of all the vital systems of our body. Research shows that 'obesity' is one of the precursors of several chronic diseases such as Hypertension, Diabetes, Thyroid disorders, PCOD etc. A correct diet advice not only helps in managing obesity but also has a beneficial effect in preventing and curing many ailments.
